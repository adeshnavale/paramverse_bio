About धरन्त्र

Institutional-Quality Biologicals.
Local Prices.

Paramverse Bio captures the ‘Missing Middle’ of Indian agriculture — between global giants with high IP but wrong strains, and unorganised local players with low cost but inconsistent results.

We combine research rigour with last-mile farmer connectivity. Indigenous, tropicalized microbial strains that perform in India’s 45°C heat, high-salinity soils, and erratic monsoon conditions.

Backed by the Paramverse Group — a BBB– rated agro-commodity organisation with established banking relationships, farmer trust across our beachhead states, and shared infrastructure that eliminates the cold-start problem.

“We do not compete with global giants on their IP. We compete on local efficacy, field presence, and the trust of the Indian farmer.”

— धरन्त्र · Competitive Philosophy

The धरन्त्र

The “Doctor of Soil” Model

Our field officers are trained, certified agronomic consultants — not salespeople. Equipped with soil test kits, a diagnostic protocol, and the authority to prescribe.

Every farm visit produces a printed Kisan Prescription — the farmer’s name and village at the top. Soil test results. A crop-specific, stage-by-stage product schedule in their regional language. QR codes on every label link to video demonstrations.

This is not a distributor network — it is a trust infrastructure. The prescription sits on the farmer’s table. It gets passed to neighbours. It becomes the reference for the entire season.
